Our Experiences

Choose your path to the sky.

Tandem Paragliding

Sit comfortably in a secure harness in front of your pilot. This is the most serene and accessible way to experience free flight. Enjoy sweeping views, gentle turns, and a smooth landing on your feet.

  • Duration: 20-30 minutes airtime
  • Intensity: Relaxing to Moderate
  • Age Limit: 6+ Years
Explore Paragliding

Tandem Hang Gliding

Fly prone (face down) strapped side-by-side with your pilot. Hang gliding offers higher speeds, sharper handling, and a true "Superman" flying sensation over the valley.

  • Duration: 15-25 minutes airtime
  • Intensity: High (Adrenaline)
  • Age Limit: 12+ Years
Explore Hang Gliding
