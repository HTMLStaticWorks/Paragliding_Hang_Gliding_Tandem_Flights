Safety is Our
First Priority

We adhere to the strictest aviation safety standards. Our equipment is inspected daily, and our pilots hold advanced master certifications.

Weight Limits

Passengers must weigh between 40kg (90lbs) and 110kg (240lbs) for safe canopy loading.

Weather Minimums

Flights require stable winds (10-25 km/h) and no incoming precipitation. We never compromise on weather.

Age & Health

Minimum age is 6 years (with guardian consent). Must be capable of a short run during takeoff.
