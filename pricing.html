Booking FAQ

Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?
No. We require a 20% deposit to secure your booking. The remainder can be paid on the day of the flight via card or cash.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel or reschedule for free up to 48 hours before your flight. Cancellations within 48 hours will forfeit the 20% deposit. If WE cancel due to weather, you get a full refund or free reschedule.
Are group discounts available?
Yes! We offer a 10% discount for groups of 4 or more booking together on the same day. Contact us directly to arrange a group booking.
