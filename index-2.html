Mission Briefing (FAQ)

What if the weather turns bad?
Extreme flights are highly weather dependent. If conditions are unsafe, we will reschedule your flight for the next available window at no additional cost.
Can I request acrobatic maneuvers?
Yes! Just let your pilot know during the basecamp briefing. Maneuvers like wingovers and deep spirals pull up to 3Gs.
What should I wear?
Dress as if you are going for a hike in cold weather. Windproof jacket, gloves, and sturdy ankle-supporting boots are mandatory.
