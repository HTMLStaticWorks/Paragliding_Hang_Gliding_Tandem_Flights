Our Origins

Born from the
Desire to Fly.

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate alpine mountaineers, AeroGlide started with a single canopy and a dream to share the unparalleled beauty of our local valleys from above.

Today, we are the region's leading luxury tandem flight operator, facilitating over 5,000 safe flights annually and introducing thousands to the purest form of aviation.

Our Mission & Standards

We don't just fly; we engineer safe memories. Our commitment to safety is uncompromising.

Our Mission

To make the dream of flight accessible to everyone while providing a premium, hassle-free, and exhilarating adventure experience.

Safety First

We utilize multi-tiered safety protocols, including daily equipment inspections, secondary reserve parachutes, and strict weather thresholds.

Eco-Conscious

Aviation powered only by wind and gravity. We maintain zero carbon footprint during our flights and protect local wildlife nesting zones.
